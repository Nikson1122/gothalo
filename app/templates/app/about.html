About Us

Revolutionizing daily construction reporting from the heart of Nepal.

Welcome to Gothalo

We are the pioneers in revolutionizing daily construction reporting, based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Our all-in-one software solution is designed to seamlessly integrate multiple aspects of construction site management, delivering unparalleled efficiency and accuracy.

What We Offer

  • Labor Attendance & Hour Tracking: Simplify attendance management with real-time tracking and hour counting, ensuring accurate payroll and workforce accountability.
  • Integrated Inventory Management: Keep your inventory in check with our streamlined billing system, tracking materials, equipment, and supplies effortlessly.
  • Comprehensive Site Reporting: Document daily site activities with ease, generating detailed reports that enhance communication, transparency, and project progress monitoring.

Our Story

Gothalo is the brainchild of Ankit Subedi, a dedicated civil engineer, and Nishant Shrestha, a talented software developer. Childhood friends with a shared vision, they combined their expertise to address the unique challenges faced by construction professionals.

The name "Gothalo" comes from the Nepalese word for someone who herds cattle or manages a barn, reflecting the app's role in managing various aspects of construction site operations with care and precision.
